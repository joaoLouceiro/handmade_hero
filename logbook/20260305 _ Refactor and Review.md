# Refactoring and reviewing
On day 5, Casey does a bit of refactoring in the app, replacing the global variables we were using with a struct, and does a review of what we have made so far. The second part is a bit more interesting.

For example, early on, he is in doubt of whether the stack grows upwards or downwards, so he does a bit of address math to find it out. Neat.

During the Q&A, he also gives us a good glimpse into the problem of pointer aliasing, around 1:50h (after much struggle).
